About

Hi! My name is David Puerta and I am a 2nd year mathematics student at Josephine Butler College, Durham. Before that I was a student at Sir Isaac Newton Sixth Form, Norwich.

On this website you will find a range of projects and resources to do with maths. From my personal university lecture notes to revision material, worksheets and links to A-Level revision videos, this website is a collection of all the projects I have worked on. If you have any questions, comments or corrections to any of my resources, don't hesitate to contact me at my email.
