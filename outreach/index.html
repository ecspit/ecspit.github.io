Outreach

Here is a collection of outreach projects I have been a part of.

Charity

We Solve Problems

We Solve Problems is a charity which develops educational materials and organises Math Circles for students with a keen intrest in mathematics. Partnered with Durham University, we delivered a series of in-person sessions focused on problem solving and creativity in mathematics to students. You can read more about this here.

University Admissions

Problem Solving Matters

Problem Solving Matters is a course for Year 12 students interested in applying to study Mathematics, Mathematical Sciences or other highly mathematics-dependent courses at University. In association with Durham Univeristy, we delivered 3 in person day sessions consisting of minature lectures, work room sessions and talks on univeristy admissions, heavily focused on cultivating problem solving skills. You can read more about it here.
