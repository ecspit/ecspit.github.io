STEP solutions

The STEP is the mathematics entrance exam used by the Univeristy of Caimbridge and some other univeristies as a part of their mathematics or maths-related courses' entry requirements. I have produced written solutions and discussions to various questions. Each document includes hints, a complete written solution and a discussion about the question. The website can be found here.
