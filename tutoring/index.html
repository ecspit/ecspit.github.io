TUTORING

Since begining to tutor GCSE Maths students in October 2023, teaching and working with young people to improve not just in mathematics but also as people has been a driving passion of mine. Here is a collection of resources and information relating to tutoring.

Tutoring Style

Specializing in 1 to 1 lessons, I provide a safe and enriching space where students are able to discuss, learn and explore.

Sessions are guided by the student's needs and questions, if the student has no outstanding queries then I work with the student through targeted past paper practice, general topic revision or even working through worksheets that I personally create and tailor to the student.
